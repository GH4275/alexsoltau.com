About Me

I recently graduated from the University of Minnesota with a B.S. in Biochemistry and Neuroscience. My research interests range from novel methods of evolving proteins to understanding cellular networks and information flow in the brain. I am working at the ICN Labs improving genetically encoded voltage indicators through machine learning-assisted protein engineering.
